# OG Image Specifications for WebnDigital

## Required Dimensions
- Width: 1200px
- Height: 630px
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Max file size: 5MB (recommended < 300KB)

## Design Guidelines

### Homepage (og-image.png)
- Background: Gradient from #0091FF to #00D4FF
- Logo: WebnDigital (top left, white)
- Main Text: "Artificial Intelligence, Make It Yours"
- Subtitle: "Custom AI Solutions & Digital Transformation"
- Visual: Abstract AI/tech patterns or neural network visualization

### Services (og-services.png)
- Background: Blue (#0091FF) with subtle pattern
- Title: "Our Services"
- Subtitle: "Web Development • AI Solutions • Digital Marketing"
- Icons: 3 service category icons in a row

### Portfolio (og-portfolio.png)
- Background: Dark gradient with blue accents
- Title: "Our Portfolio"
- Subtitle: "Success Stories & Case Studies"
- Visual: Grid of 4 project thumbnails

### Blog (og-blog.png)
- Background: Clean white with blue accent
- Title: "WebnDigital Magazine"
- Subtitle: "Insights on AI, Web Dev & Digital Transformation"
- Visual: Abstract article/document icons

### About (og-about.png)
- Background: Professional gradient
- Title: "About WebnDigital"
- Subtitle: "Leading AI & Digital Transformation Experts"
- Visual: Team illustration or office environment

### Contact (og-contact.png)
- Background: Blue gradient
- Title: "Get In Touch"
- Subtitle: "Free Consultation • London, UK"
- Visual: Contact icons (phone, email, location)

## Tools for Creation
1. Canva (easiest)
2. Figma (professional)
3. Photoshop (advanced)

## Color Palette
- Primary Blue: #0091FF
- Light Blue: #00D4FF
- Dark: #1A1A1A
- White: #FFFFFF
- Gray: #6B7280

## Typography
- Heading: IBM Plex Sans Bold, 72px
- Subtitle: IBM Plex Sans Medium, 36px
- Brand: IBM Plex Sans SemiBold, 48px

## Important Notes
- Always include WebnDigital logo
- Keep text readable at small sizes
- Test on Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn previews
- Ensure high contrast for accessibility
